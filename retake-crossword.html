Sprunki Retake Crossword mod

Sprunki Retake Crossword is a clever twist on the classic action — combining fast-paced sprunki gameplay with word puzzle challenges! In this mod, you’ll jump, dodge, and battle while unlocking hidden letters to solve crossword clues. Every correct answer gives you a boost, while mistakes trigger funny obstacles. It’s a brain-teasing, action-packed experience designed for curious minds and quick fingers. Ideal for kids who love games *and* puzzles — this version tests both reflexes and smarts!
