🌈 Sprunki Mustard mod - The Weirdest and Wildest Mod

Sprunki Mustard is a bizarre, colorful, and absolutely unpredictable mod in the Sprunki Retake universe. Bursting with chaotic visuals, random sound effects, and oddball characters, this version is all about fun, confusion, and pure meme energy.

In Sprunki Mustard, nothing works the way you expect — gravity flips, controls reverse, and levels are packed with strange surprises that will keep you laughing and guessing. It's perfect for players who enjoy humor, randomness, and totally unique challenges.

Like all Sprunki games, this mod runs in your browser with no download or sign-up. Whether you're a kid or a chaos-loving teen, Sprunki Mustard delivers wild fun you won’t forget!
